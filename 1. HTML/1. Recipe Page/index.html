Braaied Pork Neck Steak

Indulge in the smoky flavours of Braaied Pork Neck Steaks paired perfectly with juicy, grilled sweetcorn

Ingredients

  • 6 pork nexk steaks
  • 2 tablespoon oil
  • 3 tablespoon Robersons Steak & Chope Spice
  • 4 fresh sweetcorn on the cob
  • salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Prepare a medium-high heat braai. Drizzle the pork Neck steaks with oil and season with Robertsons Steak and Chops Spice
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and add sweetcorn. Boil for 10 min
  3. Place the pork neck steaks and the sweetcorn on the braai, flip the steaks every minute of so for about 8 - 12 minutes
  4. Rotate the weetcorn occasionally to ensure they char evenly
  5. Dollop the weetcorn with margarine and garnish with chopped chives.
